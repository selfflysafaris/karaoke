How It Works
Everything you need to know in 30 seconds
🔍
Search for your song
Type an artist name or song title in the search bar. The list filters as you type. Tap any song to select it.
🎤
Submit your request
Tap REQUEST THIS SONG, confirm your name, then hit SUBMIT. Your request goes straight to the DJ.
🔢
See your queue number
After submitting, you'll see your position in the queue. The number updates automatically — you don't need to stay on this screen.
⏳
Wait for your turn
The DJ works through the queue in order. Position updates stop after 22 minutes to save your battery — tap ? anytime to check again.
💡
Tips
You can request more than one song — just search and submit again. Your name is remembered for the whole evening. A friend can use your phone under a different name.
